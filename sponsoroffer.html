Attend and Participate in Competitions

ISTS (Spring) - Information Security Talent Search. A red team of industry professionals attacks blue teams from all over the country for 2 days. This competition also includes CTF and King of The Hill components, as well as the ability for blue teams to attack each other. This competition is entirely student-run and built. Sponsors are invited to the event and are encouraged to participate
IRSeC (Fall) - A 1-day, red vs blue competition with a heavy focus on incident response and reporting. It features business tasks focused on logging, threat hunting, and email responses to give a corporate feel. This competition is great for beginners and is internal to RIT. Each blue team has a sponsor as their blue team Coach.
CTF (Spring) - This is a student-run international event. Challenges are made by students and sponsors throughout the year. 900+ teams compete to earn the most points by solving challenges. Teams of all RIT students are eligible to win prizes.
Hacker-Thon (Fall) - We spend 24 hours creating a security related tool or project. Tech Talks are presented throughout the competition by upperclassmen and sponsors. This is a great way for our members to find a side project or dedicate time to one they already have.
